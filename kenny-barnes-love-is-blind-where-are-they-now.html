    What Happened To Kenny Barnes From 'Love Is Blind'?

    29 May 2024, 00:00

    Original content on Netflix can turn into a hit in the blink of an eye, which is why shows of all genres are flocking to the streaming giant. If released at the right time, a show can blow up, and this is precisely what happened with Love is Blind.

    Not everyone got to find love, but the ones that did went on a wild ride all in the name of entertainment. The contestants on the show made it a hit, and they've had plenty to say about one another since the season debuted.

    A lot has changed since the show's first season, and fans want an update on key cast members, including Kenny Barnes. Let's see what he's been up to.

    Kenny Barnes Was Featured On 'Love Is Blind' Season One

    In the earlier part of 2020, people suddenly found themselves with a surplus of Netflix binge time, and one of the hottest shows to emerge from that time was Love is Blind. While there were plenty of other dating shows for people to watch, this series hit Netflix at just the right time and became a massive hit with fans.

    Featuring a group of hopeful singles getting to know one another without actually meeting face-to-face, this show was all about establishing an emotional connection before getting engaged and marching towards a wedding date. The idea was brilliant, and fans were hooked from the show's first episode.

    Kenny Barnes was a contestant on the show, and he got engaged to Kelly Chase. The duo's chemistry was solid, and they were heading in the right direction all throughout the show.

    As fans came to learn, however, things weren't really what they seemed on camera.

    Related: 'Love Is Blind' Season 2 Episode 3 Review: 'Love In Paradise'

    Kenny And Kelly Didn't Get Married

    When it came down to making the final decision to stay together, Kenny and Kelly went their separate ways. The duo had feelings for one another and seemed to be heading in the right direction, but they were not meant to last.

    After the fallout of their engagement, Kenny opened up about his approach to the show, which included not getting married at the end.

    "We were adamant about we’re not going to get married. And really, the engagement was just to extend the experiment. And we were both committed to that," Kenny revealed.

    Kelly and Kenny were planning on remaining a couple, but Kenny pulled away, which came as a surprise to Kelly.

    "I actually wrote him a handwritten letter, not like saying like, ‘I’m in love with you,’ not crazy, but just like, ‘I really enjoyed getting to know you, and I do love you and I want to continue loving getting to know you even more.' I never got a response from it, which maybe just freaked him out, but I was just releasing a lot of emotions," she said.

    Fans were sad to see that Kelly and Kenny didn't make it last, but they will be happy to know that things have gotten much better for Barnes.

    Related: 'Love Is Blind' Season 2 Episode 2 Review: 'Love Triangles'

    Kenny Barnes Ended Up Finding Love Elsewhere

    So, what's the latest with Love is Blind's Kenny Barnes? Well, while he did not find true love on the show, he did lock down the love of his life away from the cameras.

    In August 2020, People reported that Kenny got engaged to his girlfriend, Alexandra Garrison.

    "On Aug. 7, the 28-year-old reality star proposed to girlfriend Alexandra Garrison at Duke Mansion in Charlotte, North Carolina, after dating a little over one year, he reveals exclusively to PEOPLE. The pair met after Barnes' appearance on the Netflix dating series, which was filmed in fall 2018," the site reported.

    Fans of Love is Blind were certainly curious to see how Kelly would react to the news, and she had some sweet words for Barnes and his bride to be.

    "OH MY GOSHH YAYYY!! That’s so exciting!!! I’m so excited for you two. My mouth dropped but in the best way!!!!! You deserve all the happiness Kenny!!!! Sending you both love and light! Hope to meet her someday," she posted on social media.

    It's really cool to see that these two have remained so cordial after their time together, and Barnes must have really appreciated the support that Kelly gave him and his fiancé.

    On the career front, Kenny is still attached to Team Lighting in Specification Sales and Lighting Control, per the company's website.

    Ultimately, things did not work out for Kenny Barnes on Love is Blind, but in the long run, he ended up with the right person at the right time.
