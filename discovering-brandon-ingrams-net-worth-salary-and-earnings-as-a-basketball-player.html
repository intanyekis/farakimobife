    Brandon Ingram, a player for the New Orleans Pelicans, has amassed a significant wealth despite his young age. Over the course of seven NBA seasons, Ingram has earned a total of $111.77 million from his contracts. His net worth is also fueled by lucrative endorsement deals, elevating his overall income. This article provides an insight into his earnings and financial ventures.

    Brandon Ingram’s Growing Net Worth

    Ingram’s estimated net worth is $12 million. His success in basketball has elevated him to a prominent position, both professionally and financially. Since his NBA debut in 2016, his fortune has experienced substantial growth. Despite the prevalence of criminal activities among the youth in his hometown of Kinston, North Carolina, Ingram remained dedicated to his sport and exhibited early signs of basketball prowess. Over the years, his net worth has seen fluctuations, much like his career trajectory. Here is an initial breakdown of his net worth over the past four years.

    Current Salary

    In November 30, 2020, Ingram entered into a five-year, $158 million contract with the New Orleans Pelicans, resulting in an annual salary of $31 million. Prior to joining the Pelicans, he had a four-year deal with the Los Angeles Lakers (2016-2019) worth $23.82 million, with an average yearly salary of $5.95 million.

    Before transitioning to the NBA, Ingram was a student-athlete which provided some financial support, although insufficient. However, since 2016, his commitment to a professional basketball career has significantly transformed his life. Ingram has played for esteemed teams such as the Los Angeles Lakers and the New Orleans Pelicans.

    Earnings Beyond the NBA

    Basketball is just one facet of the 2020 NBA All-Star’s earnings. He is involved in additional ventures and sponsorships that contribute millions to his wealth. Ingram is also venturing into fashion design, with plans to launch his own venture, BXI Apparel. The brand’s Instagram profile hints at the upcoming launch of Ingram’s fashion line.

    Endorsements

    Aside from his basketball career and upcoming fashion business, Ingram earns from sponsorships. He secured a substantial deal with Adidas at the onset of his NBA journey in 2016. This, in addition to a sneaker sponsorship with Jordan in August 2023, has further augmented his net worth.
