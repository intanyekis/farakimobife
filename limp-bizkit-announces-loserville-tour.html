    In an unexpected collaboration, Limp Bizkit’s frontman Fred Durst has made a guest appearance on the new song “So Raus” by German rapper Alligatoah.

    The single, released on December 3, features Durst rapping in English, while Alligatoah, whose real name is Lukas Strobel, showcases his native German.

    The music video for “So Raus” plays on the narrative of Alligatoah’s rumored “demise.” It depicts an associate, BattleBoi Basti, receiving the rapper’s last will and testament.

    In a surprising twist, Alligatoah’s final wish is revealed to be a collaboration with Fred Durst, leading to the unexpected guest appearance on the track.

    Limp Bizkit Announces “Loserville” Tour

    Limp Bizkit, the iconic rap-rock band led by Fred Durst, has exciting news for fans as they announce the “Loserville” tour for 2024. The tour, comprising 24 dates, will feature special guests Bones with Eddy Baker and Zavier Wulf, N8NOFACE, and Corey Feldman.

    Riff Raff will take on the roles of host and MC for the tour, adding an extra layer of entertainment. The band will be performing at Oak Mountain Amphitheatre in Pelham on August 9, 2024.

    Tickets for the Pelham concert are set to go on sale on Friday, December 8, at 10 a.m. CT via Live Nation/Ticketmaster, following a round of pre-sales.

    Prices for tickets range from $39.50 to $149.50, offering fans various options to experience the electrifying performance.

    The show is scheduled to begin at 6:30 p.m., promising an evening filled with high-energy music and entertainment.
