    Who Are Stassi and Patrick?

    Stassi Schroeder, born on June 24, 1988, is a versatile American television personality, podcast host, fashion blogger, model, and author. She has two younger brothers, Hunter and Nikolai, and a younger sister named Georgianna. Stassi’s grandmother, Rosemary, held a special place in her heart, even though she passed away in May 2019.

    At the age of 11, Stassi started taking drama classes at Mount Carmel Academy, her high school. When she turned 18, she embarked on a journey to Hollywood and enrolled at Loyola Marymount University. She eventually earned a bachelor’s degree in English composition.

    On the other side of the story, we have Patrick Meagher. Born on March 2, 1979, in Michigan, Patrick’s early life remains relatively private. Raised by his mother and two sisters after his parent’s separation, not much is known about his upbringing and education.

    Patrick’s career took off as a radio program host on Sirius Satellite XM radio, where he hosted a show called “Cocktails with Patrick.” The show gained popularity among radio listeners, leading to Patrick’s rise to fame. He later transitioned to Mad Dog Sports Radio, hosting “The Wrap” on Channel 85.

    The Love Story

    Stassi and Patrick’s love story was far from ordinary. They had their fair share of highs and lows, and the world watched as their relationship unfolded on reality TV. Their four-year anniversary in August would turn out to be a pivotal moment.

    According to Stassi herself, their relationship had always been a bit rocky, marked by breakups and makeup. But this time, it felt different. It was a genuine separation, and it hit Stassi hard.

    Over the next few months, she went through a challenging period as she tried to move on from the breakup. Despite the pain, Stassi didn’t cut ties with Patrick completely.     Moving On
