Well, it was great until the mule begins to slide off a few seconds later. Then, there’s nothing but regret and remorse for sliding on into them.

    Finding the balance between chic and comfortable feels impossible at times. It’s a tangled web that we are continuously weaving. If a shoe is chic, it’s uncomfortable. If it’s wearable, it’s never stylish. Our simple solution? This mule with a strap, of course!

It doesn’t matter if the mule is easy-to-wear or incorporates a slip-on silhouette. It’s actually useless if the slip-on, easy-to-wear mule doesn’t even stay on. That’s why most of Us keep an arm’s length distance with mules most times.
