    Brad And Angelina Are Close To Settling Their Legal Disagreement

    Brad knows a thing or two about messy divorces, as he’s still battling his ex Angelina Jolie for custody of their minor kids, despite being legally declared single years ago (and initially separating following a private jet incident in 2015). One recent report said that Brad and Angelina are close to coming to an agreement, after their kids begged them to put an end to the years-long battle.

    “The negotiation process was agonizing, but the financial docs were filed in the last couple of weeks,” an insider dished. “Brad just wants to move on with his life, and this final step symbolizes relief and a definitive end to their relationship.”

    “[He] agreed because it wasn’t as bad as Angie [getting] full custody like she wanted,” they went on, alleging Brad doesn’t have primary custody despite challenging Angie’s request.

    There have been rumors that Brad’s daughter Shiloh hopes to move in with him to strengthen their bond. If reports prove to be true, Brad seems to be on tenous terms with several of his older kids. Nevertheless, he may be looking to expand his family, as one insider claims he’s hoping for kids with Ines.

    “Brad and Ines are smitten with each other and she recently moved into his Los Feliz mansion, proving how serious they are,” a source told Closer Magazine. “He’s known from the outset that Ines wants to become a mum and that’s something they discussed very early on, given their 30-year age gap.”

    While Ines was seemingly “surprised” by Brad’s “willingness to become a dad again […] he insisted it was something he very much wants too.”

    “After everything he’s been through with his children, he’s keen to make a fresh start and create new memories,” they added. “Brad’s not getting any younger, so it’s something they would like sooner rather than later. He’s even joked about doing the school run in his 70s and hopes that he’ll still be seen as a heartthrob!"

    For now, the reports concerning Brad and Ines’ relationship remain unconfirmed.
