    David Robert Joseph Beckham OBE, born on May 2, 1975, in England, used to be a professional football player. Now, he’s the president and co-owner of Inter Miami and co-owner of Salford City.

    Beckham was famous for his excellent passing, crossing skills, and the way he could bend free-kicks, especially when he played as a right winger.

    Many consider him one of the finest midfielders of his era and one of the top experts in taking set pieces.

    People even regard him as one of the best footballers in the history of English football due to his talent and accomplishments for both his club and his country.

    So, he has always had a spotlight on him.

    Recently, David Beckham has been involved in a love affair scandal, with rumors even suggesting that he has a new wife.

    Did David Beckham Marry A New Wife? Rebecca Loos Story

    Just a few days ago, David and his wife opened up about his alleged affairs with his assistant, Rebecca Loos.

    This has even led to rumors speculating that David Beckham might get a new wife soon.

    However, here’s everything that happened.

    In 2003, fans saw David Beckham with a brunette at a Madrid nightclub, who they identified as Rebecca Loos, his assistant in Madrid.

    On April 4, 2004, the UK’s News of the World newspaper published a story claiming David had an affair with Rebecca.

    Later that month, she gave an extensive television interview on Sky News with Kay Burley. She revealed that they first slept together the night they met at the nightclub in September.

    Following her revelation, two more women came forward, stating they had affairs with Beckham.

    On April 11, model Sarah Marbeck said that she had an affair with Beckham in July 2001 while he was in Singapore with Manchester United.

    A week later, Celina Laurie came forward to the British tabloid The People. She told of having a one-night stand with David Beckham in Denmark in August 2002.

    In October 2004, a fourth woman claimed she had been involved with David Beckham sexually.

    Finally, in 2023, Victoria Beckham revealed that this period was “the hardest period” in their marriage.

    In a new four-part Netflix documentary series titled Beckham, Victoria said that she did resent him at that time, but they got through it together somehow.

    Additionally, the rumor that David Beckham is getting a new wife seems to be just a false rumor.

    David Beckham Married For Over Two Decades

    In 1997, David Beckham started dating Victoria Adams after she watched a Manchester United match.

    She was famous as “Posh Spice” in the Spice Girls, one of the world’s top pop groups at that time.

    Their relationship got a lot of attention from the media. People started calling them “Posh and Becks.”

    David proposed to Victoria on January 24, 1998, in a restaurant in Cheshunt, England. Their first child was born about fourteen months later.

    On July 4, 1999, they tied the knot at Luttrellstown Castle in Ireland. David’s teammate Gary Neville was his best man, and their four-month-old son, Brooklyn, carried the wedding rings.

    The ceremony was private, but some newspapers managed to get photos showing them sitting on fancy chairs.

    David and Victoria Beckham have four children: their sons Brooklyn Joseph, Romeo James, and Cruz David, and their only daughter Harper Seven.

    Now, in 2023, they have been together for more than two decades, which showcases their love for each other.
