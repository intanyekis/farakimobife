    Taylor Swift is unfazed that her ex Joe Jonas is already dating actress Camilla Belle, who starred in his "Lovebug" video.

    "They've been together since we broke up," Swift told Usmagazine.com at Wednesday's CMA Awards in Nashville. "That's why we broke up – because he met her."

    The 18-year-old singer — who recently revealed how Jonas dumped her in a 27-second phone call — added, "We don't talk."

    So how does Swift get over her exes?

    "Write songs about it," said Swift, who penned the tune "Always and Forever" about her split with Jonas. Do guys ever get intimidated by that? "I think they are starting to realize that if they are involved with me in any way, I will probably write a song about them," she added. "You know what, if they don't want me to write bad songs about them, they shouldn't do bad things."
