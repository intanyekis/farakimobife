    The Leicester, Leicestershire-born basketball player Nikola Jović plays for the Miami Heat of the National Basketball Association (NBA). 

    The 20-year-old young athlete previously played for Mega Basket, a youth system. At the 2020-21 Euroleague Basketball Next Generation Tournament, he averaged 29.3 points and 10.3 rebounds, winning the MVP award.

    During his ABA league career, he secured multiple victories and titles, including the 2022 Top Prospect title. Before making his NBA debut, he even played for the Serbia U19 national basketball team.

    Later, Miami Heat drafted him as the 27th overall pickup in the 2022 NBA Draft. On July 2, 2022, he officially signed a multi-year rookie scale contract with the Heat, beginning his NBA journey.
