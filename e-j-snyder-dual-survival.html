    When the ratings for Dual Survival took a nosedive, the Discovery network decided to change the hosts of the series. In their defense, the strategy had worked in the past seasons. But instead of looking for new stars, the network plucked two of their best stars from Naked and Afraid, EJ Snyder and Jeff Zausch. Both stars were well received and had made a name for themselves. On paper, things worked out perfectly. But in reality, nothing could be farther from the truth. The pair failed to find any chemistry and the series fell apart. In this article, we will take a closer look at the wiki bio of EJ Snyder. Also, we will delve into his personal life aspects like his marriage and children.

    Wiki/Bio Facts

    Upbringing & Family

    Errol James Snyder Jr. was born on the 3rd of December, 1965, in Englewood, New Jersey, U.S. Born in the suburbs of New Jersey, EJ had a tough time growing up. He and his late brother Jeff were raised by their single mother. His mother undertook three different jobs just to make ends meet. Errol barely talks about his father and little is known about him. On the other hand, Errol’s brother Jeff passed away in 1986, Just before Errol’s 22nd birthday. EJ attended Ranger School and received his first survival training. He later attended the Army Survival school and started working as a survival expert.

    Life in the Army

    EJ decided to join the army when he was just 19 years of age. This was back in 1984. EJ served for 25 years in the army and during which, EJ served the infantry and was also a part of the airborne units. EJ was deployed in the 1991 Gulf War and was a part of the 2004 Operation: Iraqi Freedom. During his time in the Army, Ej was nicknamed the “Skullcrusher.” Over the years, EJ served as the Survival and Tracking Instructor, Drill Sergeant, and Ranger Instructor. During his army days, EJ was often brought in to advise on leadership, building shelter, Tracking, navigation, self-defense, trapping, and the use of primitive weapons.

    Back home

    Errol retired from the army in 2009 but quickly started making a name for himself through TV shows. He made his first appearance in Patton 360 (2009) which attempted to uncover the life of the military icon, George S. Patton. He then starred in two reality TV series, Naked and Afraid and Dual Survival. EJ paired up with Kellie Nightlinger for the second episode of the very first season. The pair had to survive in Tanzania’s Serengeti Plain. The episode aired on the 30th of June, 2013, and was titled, Terror in Tanzania.

    Dual Survival

    EJ was greeted with rave reviews from fans and critics alike. Everyone was impressed with EJ’s quick thinking and skills to put the thinking into action. So when the ratings of Dual Survival started going down, EJ was called into action. He had to pair up with another Naked and Afraid survivor, Jeff Zausch. Even though great things were expected from the pair, there was no real chemistry and the series closed shop in 2016.

    Naked and Afraid, Naked & Afraid XL:

    EJ had a short-lived career in Dual Survival, he appeared on season 9  which turned out to be the last. However, even before he set foot on the set of “Dual Survival,” he was a star. All thanks to his time on “Naked & Afraid,” where participants have to endure the test of nature and survive the wilderness naked. EJ appeared in a total of 7 episodes from 2013-2020. Following a similar premise in Naked and Afraid XL, EJ after debuting in 2015 appeared in 30 episodes until 2022.

    EJ Snyder is not just a survivalist. He is also an actor, producer, and location manager. He played Redfern on Lost and Max on Survival T.V. The Movie! He is a versatile and talented entertainer.

    Controversies

    Ej has also faced some controversies and criticisms during his career. The first controversy is related to Snyder’s childhood bullying. According to his biography on Wikipedia, Snyder experienced bullying when he was growing up in North Jersey. This motivated him to become interested in strength training and self-defense. However, some critics have accused him of being a bully himself on the shows he participated in. For example, on Naked and Afraid XL, he clashed with several other survivalists over leadership, resources, and strategies. He also had a heated argument with Matt Wright. Some viewers felt that Snyder was too aggressive, arrogant, and domineering towards his teammates.

    The second controversy is related to Snyder’s scrotum injury. On the sixth season of Naked and Afraid XL, Snyder suffered a gruesome injury when he fell from a tree branch into the swamp. The fall resulted in severe lacerations to his scrotum, which required eight stitches by the show’s medical team. Snyder was given the option to leave the challenge and seek medical treatment at a hospital, or stay in the game and risk infection and the loss of his genitals. He chose to stay and continue the 60-day challenge. However, some critics have questioned his decision and called it reckless, irresponsible, and foolish. They argued that he put his health and life at risk for the sake of a television show.

    Net Worth

    A wise man knows how to make the most of the situation, and EJ seems to be in sync with the statement. Apart from survival, EJ brings his A-game to marketing. He has amassed over 202K followers on his Facebook page, and 239K followers on Instagram. EJ has set up his personal website, “ejsnyder.com,” where he sells digital courses on survival, one can personally book him to speak and can join him for survival courses. With different endeavors, EJ has an estimated net worth of $1.5 M as of 2023.

    Marital Status

    EJ Snyder is a married man. He tied the knot with his longtime girlfriend Amy, who has also been featured in Naked and Afraid. The pair has two lovely kids, a son Tyler and a daughter, Kassidy. EJ is close to his children and loves spending time with them.
